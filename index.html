Summary: Latitude vs. X

This study compares various weather factors vs. distance from the equator. Data was pulled via the OpenWeather API with a data set size of 594 locations. Date of the data was October 21, 2020

Summary of Trends Observed:

  1. 1)On average, Temperature increases as locations near the equator.
  2. 2)Humidity and Cloudiness show little correlation with latitude.
  3. 3)This study is based on a single snapshot in time, local weather factors, as well as seasonality may also play a significant factor. (ie. late fall in Northern Hemishpere vs. Late Spring in Southern)
